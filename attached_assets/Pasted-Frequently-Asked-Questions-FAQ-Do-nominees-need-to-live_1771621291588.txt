Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)
Do nominees need to live in Hawaii to participate?

No. The HI FIT Competition is a national platform, and nominees may be located anywhere within the United States. Individuals, brands, and businesses from any state are welcome to be nominated and compete.

Can only Hawaii residents vote in the competition?

No. Voting is open nationally, and supporters may participate from anywhere. Nominees are encouraged to rally votes from friends, fans, customers, and communities across the country.

How many free votes can I cast?

Each participant may cast one free vote per category per day. This allows supporters to consistently show encouragement throughout the competition.

Can additional votes be purchased?

Yes. Vote packages are available for those who wish to show extra support. There is no limit to the number of votes that may be purchased.

Is there a limit on how often I can vote?

Free votes reset daily, and participants may return each day to support their favorites. Purchased votes may be submitted at any time.

When does voting end?

Voting remains open until the week prior to the HI FIT Expo. The official closing date will be announced in advance, and categories will then be locked.

What happens after voting closes?

The top three highest-voted nominees in each category will be notified of their finalist status. Winners are announced live during the HI FIT Expo Award Show.

Do finalists need to attend the HI FIT Expo?

While attendance is not mandatory, finalists are strongly encouraged to attend the Award Show for recognition and celebration. Certain prizes and media opportunities may require presence at the event.

What is the nomination fee?

The nomination fee is $5 per submission. This fee helps support platform operations, promotional visibility, and competition administration.

Who can submit a nomination?

Anyone may nominate a deserving individual, brand, or business. Nominators simply complete the nomination form and provide basic information about the nominee.

Can someone be nominated more than once?

Each nominee may only be entered once per category to ensure fairness. Duplicate submissions may be reviewed or removed.

How will nominees be notified?

Nominees receive an automatic email notification once their nomination is completed. This email includes instructions for completing their competition profile.

What can nominees upload to their profile?

Nominees may upload 1–10 photos and up to 4 videos to represent themselves, their achievements, or their brand.

Can nominees promote their participation?

Absolutely. Nominees are encouraged to share their competition page and official marketing flyer across social media and networks to gain support.

How does the non-profit sponsorship work?

Each nominee selects a non-profit organization they wish to sponsor. Winning nominees help generate contributions and awareness for their chosen cause.

Is voting secure and monitored?

Yes. The competition platform tracks voting activity to ensure fairness and integrity. Any suspicious or abusive activity may be reviewed.

Are nomination or voting fees refundable?

All fees are final. Due to the nature of digital competition systems and promotional visibility, payments are non-refundable.

How are winners determined?

Winners are determined by total votes accumulated within each category during the official voting period.

How can I increase my nominee’s chances of winning?

Consistent daily voting, social sharing, community engagement, and supporter participation all play a major role in competition success.
